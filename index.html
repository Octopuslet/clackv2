Protection

Everything uploaded to the website will be thoroughly scanned and tested by us before being uploaded to the website.
Our customer's well-being is our top priority.
In case a product is uploaded and contains malicious contents within it,
report it to the staff immediately.

Community

In order to have a connection with creators and customers we constantly take feedback and design ideas on the website.
New features and design ideas might be implemented on the website after proper approval from company owners.
Every 6 months the design team hosts a event that lets artist give their look/vision on the website and what it could look like.
Winner of the competition will win a cash prize determined by our team.
(Winner's look/vision might or might not be implemented.)
